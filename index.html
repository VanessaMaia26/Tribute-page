Bill Gates

About

Bill Gates is an American tycoon, entrepreneur, CEO, investor, philanthropist and author, best known for founding Paul Allen to Microsoft, the world's largest and best-known software company in terms of market value.

Gates currently serves as the non-executive chairman of Microsoft, in addition to being regularly ranked as the richest person in the world, a position he held from 1995 to 2007, 2009, and from 2014 to 2017. He is one of the pioneers in the personal computer revolution.

" I believe that if you show people the problems and you show them the solutions they will be moved to act. "
